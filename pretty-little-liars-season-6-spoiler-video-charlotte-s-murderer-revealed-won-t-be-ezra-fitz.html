Pretty Little Liars Season 6 Spoiler Video: Charlottes Murderer Revealed Wont Be Ezra Fitz

August 08, 2024
blog

Pretty Little Liars spoilers are rolling in – and all signs are pointing to Ezra Fitz as being the new “Big Bad” of Season 6. Freeform (formerly ABC Family) has rolled out an all new spoiler video for the upcoming episode which is titled “New Guys, New Lies.”

In the PLL spoiler video, Aria (Lucy Hale), Hanna (Ashley Benson), Emily (Shay Mitchell), and Spencer (Troian Belisario) are sitting down for a pow-wow and arguing over whether or not Ezra is Charlotte DiLaurentis’ killer.

Pretty Little Liars spoilers tease that Aria Montgomery (Lucy Hale) will be standing by her man (or ex-man) and insists to her friends that Ezra is innocent. But, the rest of the liars aren’t buying it – and for good reason. After all, the Ezra (Ian Harding) we know now is not the same kind and gentle teacher that used to grace the halls of Rosewood.

The new Ezra has a drinking problem, violent outbursts, and even Hanna’s (Ashley Benson) mother revealed he had an “incident” at the hotel. Then there is the fact that he was out roaming the streets of Rosewood the night that Charlotte (Vanessa Ray) was killed – and he won’t give anyone a straight answer about his exact whereabouts.

In the all new Season 6 spoiler video, Aria argues with Spencer that Ezra “said he didn’t do it.” Spencer snaps back, “Maybe that’s what you heard, but the rest of us heard Ezra kick us out of his apartment.” Meanwhile, Aria is trying to shoulder the blame on to Alison DiLaurentis (Sasha Pieterse), she tries to convince her friends that Alison is their new cyber-stalker trying to get them to rat out Charlotte’s killer.

All Pretty Little Liars spoilers are pointing to Ezra being Charlotte’s killer – and that is exactly why we think Ezra is innocent. This isn’t our first time at the rodeo with the PLL writers, and it is way too early in the season and way too obvious.

It’s Season 6, and we have been down this road time and time again, the obvious suspect is always just a red herring and within a few episodes will most likely be cleared of any suspected wrong-doings.

So, if Ezra isn’t the “Big Bad” or Charlotte’s killer, then who is? Well, we’d never rule out Mona Vanderwaal, she’s still snooping around Rosewood and doesn’t seem to be going anywhere any time soon. Also, she testified to have Charlotte set free at her hearing – maybe she wanted her free so she could kill her?

Alison definitely is tied up in this mess some how, if she doesn’t know who killed Charlotte – then she most definitely does know who the cyber stalker is, or she may even be the cyber stalker.
