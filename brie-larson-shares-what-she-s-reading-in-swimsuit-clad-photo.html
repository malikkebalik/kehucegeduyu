When Brie Larson isn’t wrapping herself in chains to keep in superhero shape as Captain Marvel, it looks like she enjoys reading a good book just like the rest of us Earthlings. However, unlike some of us, she does so in style in an fabulous hot tub.

It seems romance may be on her mind as the book covers topics like, “How do we find love? How do we sustain it? And how do we survive when we lose it?” according to its description.
