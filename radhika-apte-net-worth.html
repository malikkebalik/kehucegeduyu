Radhika Apte is an Indian actress. Her acting career started in the mid-2000s. She made her debut in the Hindi movie Vaah! Life Ho Toh Aisi!. Her next role came some years later, starring in the Bengali film Antaheen. She next starred in the Marathi movies Samaantar and Gho Mala Asla Hava. She then rose to fame for starring in the movie Rakht Charitra I and Rakht Charitra II. Subsequently, she starred in the movie Dhoni. In 2015, she starred in the Bollywood movie Badlapur, which got her praise. She was also widely acclaimed for her performance in 2016’s Parched.
