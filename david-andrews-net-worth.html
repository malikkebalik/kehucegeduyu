David Andrews is an American actor. He first appeared in the 1984 film A Nightmare on Elm Street. He next had roles in the TV films The Burning Bed and Wild Horses before starring in the 1987 film Cherry 2000. He continued his acting career in the 1990s, landing roles in such movies as Graveyard Shift, Wyatt Earp, and Bad Day on the Block. He also portrayed Pete Conrad in Apollo 13 and played Thomas in Fight Club. The following decade, Andrews had performances in the films Hannibal, A Walk to Remember, Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines, and Stealth. In the 2010s, some of his film credits include Dear John, The Conspirator, Arthur Newman, and Jessabelle.
