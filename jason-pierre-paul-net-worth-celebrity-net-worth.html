    Jason Pierre-Paul is an American professional football player who has a net worth of $35 million. Jason Pierre-Paul was born in Deerfield Beach, Florida in January 1989. He is a defensive end who started playing football after he injured his leg playing basketball in high school. Pierre-Paul played at the University of South Florida where he was named First-team All-American and First-team All-Big East in 2009. Pierre-Paul was drafted #15 by the New York Giants in the 2010 NFL Draft and has played for the Giants his entire career.

Pierre-Paul was seriously injured on July 4, 2015 in a fireworks accident and had a finger amputated.
