What is Alexandre Rockwell's Net Worth?

Alexandre Rockwell is an American film director, producer, and screenwriter who has a net worth of $20 million. Alexandre Rockwell was born in Boston, Massachusetts in August 1956. He wrote and directed the films Hero in 1983, Sons in 1990, In the Soup in 1992, and Somebody to Love in 1994.
